## Usage Notes

### Choosing the Right Template

- **Problem-Solution**: Best for addressing specific pain points
- **Case Study**: When you have concrete results to share (anonymized)
- **Technical Guide**: For decision-making frameworks and evaluation criteria
- **Myth-Busting**: When you want to challenge common but wrong assumptions

### Customization Tips

1. **Always adapt to your specific topic** - these are frameworks, not fill-in-the-blank forms
2. **Keep the structure** but change the content completely
3. **Maintain the tone** - direct, helpful, expert without being salesy
4. **Use real examples** even if anonymized
5. **Include specific numbers** wherever possible

### Quality Check

Before publishing using any template:
- [ ] Changed all placeholder text
- [ ] Added specific examples from your experience
- [ ] Included measurable outcomes or data points
- [ ] Maintained conversational, direct tone
- [ ] CTA feels natural, not forced
- [ ] Reader gets actionable value
