# Cover Image Prompts — Blog Post Writer Skill

Professional editorial-style image prompts for LinkedIn-ready blog post covers.

## Visual Brand Guidelines

**Editorial Style:**
- Modern business magazine aesthetic (Wired, Fast Company, Harvard Business Review)
- Cinematic, dramatic lighting
- High-end 3D renders or premium photography
- Professional, polished, premium quality
- Optimized for LinkedIn sharing

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Rich darks (#1A1A1A, #2D2D2D)
- Secondary: Clean whites (#FFFFFF, #FAFAFA)
- Accent: Gold (#B8860B) used strategically
- Depth: Subtle gradients, shadows, dimension

**Style DNA:**
- Editorial magazine quality
- Tech product photography aesthetic
- Cinematic depth and lighting
- Professional business context
- LinkedIn-optimized composition
- Premium, shareable content

**What to AVOID:**
- Generic stock photos
- Cheap-looking renders
- Flat, amateur lighting
- Busy, cluttered composition
- Low-resolution images
- Abstract geometric patterns that don't translate well

**What to INCLUDE:**
- Professional editorial photography
- Dramatic side/directional lighting
- Real-world tech elements (screens, devices, workspaces)
- Bold, clean typography when appropriate
- Premium finish and polish
- LinkedIn-worthy composition

## Customization Guidelines

When adapting these prompts:

1. **Keep editorial quality:**
   - Premium photography/render quality
   - Dramatic, cinematic lighting
   - Professional business context
   - LinkedIn-ready composition

2. **Adjust the scene:**
   - Match visual concept to post topic
   - Use real-world professional elements
   - Maintain dramatic lighting setup

3. **Focus on shareability:**
   - Images must look premium on LinkedIn
   - Professional business magazine aesthetic
   - High production value visible at thumbnail size
   - Clear, sophisticated composition

4. **Test against editorial standards:**
   - Would this appear in Wired or Fast Company?
   - Is it LinkedIn-worthy for a B2B professional?
   - Does it have cinematic lighting and depth?
   - Would you be proud to share this?

## AI Tool Recommendations

**Primary: Midjourney V6**
- Best for editorial photography style
- Excellent with lighting and composition
- Use `--ar 1.91:1` for correct aspect ratio
- Use `--style raw` for photorealistic results
- Add keywords: "professional photography", "editorial style", "cinematic lighting"

**Example Midjourney command:**
```
[your prompt] --ar 1.91:1 --style raw --v 6 --q 2
```

**Alternative: DALL-E 3**
- Good for consistent results
- Specify "professional photography, editorial magazine quality"
- Request "cinematic lighting, dramatic shadows"

**For 3D Renders: Leonardo.ai**
- Excellent for product-style shots
- Good control over lighting
- Premium quality outputs

**Post-processing (Essential):**
- **Photoshop/Lightroom** - Adjust exposure, contrast, color grading
- **Canva Pro** - Add subtle overlays if needed, adjust colors
- **TinyPNG/Squoosh** - Compress to < 200KB without visible quality loss

**Color Grading Tips:**
- Enhance dramatic shadows
- Boost contrast slightly
- Add subtle gold tint to highlights
- Maintain rich, dark tones
- Keep it sophisticated, not oversaturated
