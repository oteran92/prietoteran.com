   - Before/after results
   - Industry-specific examples
   - Lessons learned

   - Best practices

## Content Planning Process

### Step 1: Data Review (Monthly)

```
1. Review Search Console data
   - Top queries driving traffic
   - Rising queries
   - Queries with opportunities

2. Review Google Analytics
   - Top performing content
   - Conversion paths
   - User behavior

3. Review market-intelligence.json
   - Pain points resonating
   - Industries showing interest
```

### Step 2: Topic Selection

**Criteria for topic selection:**
- Search volume potential (GSC impressions)
- Relevance to services
- Ability to demonstrate expertise
- Gap in current content
- Supports sales conversations

**Topic validation questions:**
1. Is someone searching for this?
2. Can we provide unique value?
3. Does it lead toward our services?
4. Is it timely/evergreen?

### Step 3: Content Mapping

```
For each piece of content, define:
- Primary keyword
- Secondary keywords
- Target audience (role)
- Content format (how-to, case study, etc.)
- CTA (what action?)
- Promotion plan (channels, timing)
```

## Blog Post Schedule

### Publishing Cadence
- **Target:** 2 blog posts per month
- **Publishing day:** Tuesday or Wednesday
- **Time:** 8:00 AM CET

### Blog Post Checklist

**Before publishing:**
- [ ] Content reviewed for quality
- [ ] SEO optimized (title, meta, headings)
- [ ] Images optimized
- [ ] Internal links added
- [ ] CTA clear
- [ ] Schema markup added

**After publishing:**
- [ ] Submit to Search Console
- [ ] Update sitemap
- [ ] Create social posts
- [ ] Update content calendar
- [ ] Share with Sales (if relevant to outreach)

## Social Media Schedule

### LinkedIn

**Frequency:** 2-3 posts per week
**Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
**Best times:** 8:00 AM, 12:00 PM CET

**Post types rotation:**
1. Blog promotion (when new post)
2. Insight/tip
3. Question/discussion
4. Case study teaser
5. Industry observation

### X (Twitter)

**Frequency:** 3-5 posts per week
**Best days:** Weekdays
**Best times:** 9:00 AM, 12:00 PM, 5:00 PM CET

**Post types rotation:**
1. Quick insight (single tweet)
2. Thread (blog summary)
3. Quote/stat
4. Tip
5. Engagement (retweet with comment)

## Campaign Planning

### Campaign Structure

```
Campaign: [Name]
Duration: [X weeks]
Goal: [Specific measurable goal]

Content pieces:
1. Cornerstone: [Blog post or guide]
2. Support: [3-5 social posts]
3. Outreach: [Email to leads about topic]

Promotion:
- Week 1: Launch cornerstone, initial social
- Week 2: Additional social angles
- Week 3: Email outreach, engagement
- Week 4: Results review, iteration
```

### Example Campaign: "Integration Failures"

```
Campaign: Why Integrations Fail
Duration: 3 weeks
Goal: 500 blog visits, 3 contact form submissions

Content pieces:
1. Cornerstone: "Why Salesforce-ERP Integrations Break" blog
2. Support:
   - LinkedIn: Problem awareness post
   - LinkedIn: 3 principles teaser
   - X: Thread with warning signs
   - X: Before/after quote
   - LinkedIn: Discussion question

Promotion:
- Week 1: Publish blog, LinkedIn promotion, X thread
- Week 2: Additional angles, engage comments
- Week 3: Sales uses for outreach, measure results
```
