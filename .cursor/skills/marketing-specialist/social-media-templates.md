## Posting Guidelines

### LinkedIn Best Practices
- Post 2-3x per week
- Best times: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM CET
- Engage with comments within first hour
- Add link in first comment (not in post)
- Use line breaks for readability
- Tag relevant people/companies when appropriate

### X Best Practices
- Post 3-5x per week
- Best times: Weekdays, 9 AM and 12 PM CET
- Use threads for longer content
- Engage with replies
- Retweet/quote relevant industry content
- Keep hashtags to 2-3 maximum
