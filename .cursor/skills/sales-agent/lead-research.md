# Lead Research Guide

## Research Sources

### Primary Sources

#### 1. LinkedIn
**Best for:** Contact identification, company info, job postings

**Research approach:**
```
1. Company Page
   - Employee count
   - Industry
   - Recent posts (company news)
   - Employee list (find decision-makers)

2. Job Postings
   - Integration/automation mentions
   - Tech stack clues
   - Growth signals

3. Decision-Maker Profiles
   - Current role verification
   - Career history (relevant experience?)
   - Activity (engaged on LinkedIn?)
   - Connections (any mutual?)
```

#### 2. Company Website
**Best for:** Business understanding, tech stack clues, contact info

**Research approach:**
```
1. About Page
   - Company description
   - History and milestones
   - Leadership team

2. Careers/Jobs Page
   - Open positions
   - Tech requirements
   - Growth indicators

3. Blog/News
   - Recent developments
   - Pain point discussions
   - Technology mentions

4. Contact Page
   - Physical locations
   - General email patterns
```

#### 3. Swiss Business Registry (zefix.ch)
**Best for:** Company verification, legal details

**Data available:**
- Legal company name
- Registration number
- Legal form (AG, GmbH, etc.)
- Registered address
- Company purpose

#### 4. Moneyhouse.ch
**Best for:** Financial indicators, company size

**Data available:**
- Revenue estimates
- Employee count
- Industry classification
- Management names

### Secondary Sources

#### Job Posting Sites
- jobs.ch
- indeed.ch
- LinkedIn Jobs

**What to look for:**
- "Integration" in job requirements
- "ERP" or "Salesforce" mentions
- "Process automation" needs
- "Data migration" projects

#### Tech Detection Tools
- BuiltWith (website technology)
- Wappalyzer (browser extension)
- Company job postings (tech requirements)

#### News & Press
- Google News (company name)
- Industry publications
- Press release sections

## Research Process

### Step 1: Quick Assessment (5 min)

**Goal:** Determine if worth deeper research

```
1. Check LinkedIn company page
   - Employee count in range?
   - B2B company?
   - Swiss location?

2. Quick website scan
   - Professional site?
   - Active business?

3. Initial tech check
   - Any CRM/ERP mentions?

Decision: Continue research or skip
```

### Step 2: Company Deep Dive (15 min)

**Goal:** Full company profile

```
1. Complete company research template
2. Verify with multiple sources
3. Document all findings
4. Calculate company fit score
```

### Step 3: Contact Identification (10 min)

**Goal:** Find best contact

```
1. List potential contacts from LinkedIn
2. Prioritize by role fit
3. Complete contact template for top 1-2
4. Calculate contact fit score
```

### Step 4: Timing Signal Check (5 min)

**Goal:** Assess urgency

```
1. Check recent job postings
2. Scan recent news/posts
3. Note any trigger events
4. Calculate timing score
```

### Step 5: Final Qualification (5 min)

**Goal:** Score and recommend action

```
1. Calculate total score
2. Make priority recommendation
3. Define next action
4. Add to leads.json
```

## Search Queries

### LinkedIn Search Patterns

**Find companies:**
```
"Salesforce" AND "Switzerland" AND "Manufacturing"
"ERP integration" AND "Zürich"
"SAP" AND "50-200 employees" AND "Switzerland"
```

**Find decision-makers:**
```
"CTO" AND "Manufacturing" AND "Switzerland"
"Operations" AND "Director" AND "Zürich"
"Head of IT" AND "[Company Name]"
```

**Find pain point signals:**
```
"integration challenges" AND "Switzerland"
"manual processes" AND "operations" AND "B2B"
"digital transformation" AND "manufacturing" AND "CH"
```

### Google Search Patterns

**Company tech stack:**
```
"[Company Name]" AND "Salesforce"
"[Company Name]" AND "SAP"
site:[company.ch] "ERP" OR "CRM"
```

**Job posting search:**
```
site:jobs.ch "[Company Name]" "integration"
site:linkedin.com/jobs "[Company Name]" "automation"
```

**News and updates:**
```
"[Company Name]" AND "digital transformation"
"[Company Name]" AND "new system" OR "implementation"
```

## Quality Standards

### Data Quality Rules

1. **Verification**
   - Cross-reference with 2+ sources
   - Mark confidence level on all data
   - Note source for each piece of info

2. **Currency**
   - Contact info < 6 months old
   - Employee count from recent source
   - Job postings < 30 days old

3. **Completeness**
   - All required fields filled
   - Research notes explain gaps
   - Next action clearly defined

### Red Flags to Note

- High employee turnover in leadership
- Recent negative press
- Lawsuit mentions
- Financial difficulties
- Layoff announcements
- Rapid leadership changes
