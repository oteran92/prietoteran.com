# Lead Qualification Criteria

## Scoring Framework

Total Score: 100 points possible
- **Company Fit:** 40 points
- **Contact Fit:** 30 points
- **Timing Signals:** 30 points

### Qualification Thresholds

| Score Range | Priority | Action |
|-------------|----------|--------|
| 80-100 | High | Immediate outreach, personalized approach |
| 60-79 | Medium | Standard outreach, follow sequence |
| 40-59 | Low | Nurture, add to newsletter |
| 0-39 | Disqualify | Do not pursue |

## Disqualification Criteria

Automatically disqualify if ANY of these are true:

### Hard Disqualifiers
- Company is B2C only
- Company has < 20 employees
- Company is outside Europe
- Contact is no longer at company
- Company is a direct competitor
- Company has filed for bankruptcy

### Soft Disqualifiers (Lower score significantly)
- Company has internal development team > 20 people
- Company is a consulting firm (potential competitor)
- Contact has "Intern" or "Assistant" in title
- Company has had negative press recently
- Contact has been unresponsive to past outreach

## Qualification Checklist

Before adding a lead to the pipeline:

### Minimum Requirements
- [ ] Company exists and is active
- [ ] Company is B2B focused
- [ ] Company size is 30+ employees
- [ ] Company is in Switzerland or DACH
- [ ] At least one technology signal present
- [ ] Contact is currently employed there
- [ ] Have at least LinkedIn profile for contact

### Quality Standards
- [ ] Score calculated and documented
- [ ] At least one pain point identified
- [ ] Research notes explain why this is a fit
- [ ] Next action clearly defined
